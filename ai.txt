For the final project, I used AI to primarily debug issues and navigate problems I was
having with the APIs I was working with.

This included when I had written all of my functions, and there were no errors, yet
there were no tracks being listed on the results page, leading to me to believe that 
there was a serious bug in the program. However, after copy-pasting my code into AI, I was
led to believe that I was not inputting the correct parameters. This was also not true.
The issue ended up being that the API endpoint was no longer available through Spotify,
which meant I needed to figure out a different way to extract tracks in Spotify.

I also used AI to double check my logic in both app.py and functions.py. While I mainly
referenced HW5 and HW6 to write the logic regarding calling APIs, building URLs, creating a
Flask app, etc, there were points where I was not sure what the syntax looked like.

I also used AI to understand the Authentication required by Spotify in order to use it's
endpoints. I would ask AI the purpose of the Authentication, why it was required, and
